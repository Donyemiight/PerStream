How does this work? Click to read
  1. Sign in with any email — your wallet is created instantly.
  2. Pick a track above. Try the 4-minute episode first — it shows the real per-second mechanic.
  3. Deposit USDC with + Add 1 USDC or + Add 5 USDC. The status flips to “Ready”.
  4. Tap ▶ Start Streaming. The audio plays AND your balance ticks down in real time.
  5. Pause the meter freezes. Resume and it continues from where you left off.
  6. Try the AI Listener below the player — an agent can listen and pay for you, then leave a review.
  7. Rate this demo at the bottom — real comments help the team score it.

Everything here is in mock mode. The real backend on Arc testnet swaps USDC for these simulated balances — the meter, x402, and per-second ticks are all the same code.
