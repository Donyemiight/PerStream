How it works

From email to on-chain in 4 steps

01
📧

Sign in with email

One-click email login. We create a Circle Arc wallet for you in milliseconds — no seed phrase, no MetaMask.

~1 second
02
💸

Fund your account

Tap "Add USDC". Testnet-funded via the seller wallet — a real Arc testnet transaction you can verify on Arcscan.

~3 seconds
03
▶️

Press play

Each second ticks. Each tick is a $0.0001 USDC micro-payment on Arc, settled via x402 + Nanopayments batching.

real-time
04
⏸

Pause anytime

The meter freezes. The payment stream stops. You only paid for the seconds you actually heard.

instant
Why PerStream

Built for the next era of audio

⚡

Real-time settlement

Every second settles on Arc via Circle Gateway. No batched payouts, no waiting. The creator sees their balance grow as you listen.

🎙️

For independent creators

From listener #1. No minimum audience. No platform cut beyond gas. Replace ads and subscriptions with honest pay-per-listen.

🔐

No wallet needed

One-click email login. Embedded wallet via Circle Agent Stack. Your listeners don't need to know what a seed phrase is.

🤖

AI Listener Agent

An agent that subscribes on behalf of listeners, streams audio, and leaves ratings. Try the agent API →
