The problem

Audio monetization is broken.

Listeners pay for hours they never hear. Creators wait weeks for payouts. Platforms take 30–50%. And nobody — nobody — has built the obvious primitive.

💸

Subscriptions waste listener money

You subscribe for one show. You listen to one show. You paid for 12. PerStream fixes that.

⏰

Creators wait for payouts

Traditional platforms batch monthly. PerStream settles every second. You earn as you teach, talk, perform.

📉

High platform fees

30–50% of revenue goes to middlemen. On PerStream, the only cost is gas — fractions of a cent.

🔐

Crypto UX is hostile

Seed phrases, gas fees, network switches. Most listeners bounce. PerStream hides all of that.

The solution

Pay for seconds. Earn from seconds.

PerStream is the world's first per-second USDC streaming paywall. It uses Circle Nanopayments and Arc testnet to settle real on-chain USDC every second audio plays.

How it works

From email to on-chain in 4 steps

The simplest UX for the most advanced payment primitive. No wallet, no seed phrase, no friction.

One-click email login. We create a Circle Arc wallet for you in milliseconds — no seed phrase, no MetaMask.
